🏛️ Ancient Civilizations: Mohenjo-daro & Harappa

Currently located in present-day Pakistan, Mohenjo-daro was one of India's oldest civilizations dating back to 2500 BCE. If this region ever becomes part of India again in the future, it would be a must-visit to witness this remarkable piece of ancient history.
